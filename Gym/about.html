Our History in the Market


Next Level Fitness, founded by Ricardo, revolutionized the market by promoting health and well-being in a welcoming and personalized way. With modern equipment and a team of highly skilled instructors offering individualized workout plans, it quickly gained the trust of clients like Mariana, who found the encouragement she needed to achieve her health goals.


The gym's success spread through word of mouth and social media, leading to the expansion of new locations across the city. By also offering workshops on nutrition and emotional well-being, Next Level Fitness established itself as a comprehensive transformation center. Through continuous innovation and a realistic commitment to clients, Next Level Fitness became a market leader in fitness, inspiring and transforming lives.

Our Services Available to the Sonsumer:


Nutritionist

At Next Level Fitness, our nutritionists are dedicated to creating personalized meal plans that meet your specific needs and goals. With a science-based approach, we ensure your diet helps maximize your fitness results and overall well-being.

Physical Assessors

Our team of physical assessors conducts detailed evaluations to understand your current fitness level and specific needs. We use cutting-edge technology and proven methods to provide an accurate diagnosis and an effective action plan.

Instructors

The instructors at Next Level Fitness specialize in a variety of training modalities, ensuring dynamic and engaging classes. Our goal is to provide a continuous and enjoyable learning experience, helping you reach new levels of performance.

Sports Coaches

Our sports coaches are former athletes and professionals with extensive experience, ready to take your performance to the next level. We offer specific training and personalized strategies to enhance your technique, endurance, and competitiveness in your sport.
